GE Medical Systems, LLC: Allia IGS 3 Pulse angiographic X-ray system Recall

GE HealthCare has become aware of a potential issue affecting an electrical cable on certain IGS systems equipped with Gaia generators. This issue could lead to a risk of electrical shock to service personnel performing installation, maintenance, or troubleshooting activities inside the C-FRT cabinet while power is ON. Product details: Allia IGS 3 Pulse angiographic X-ray system
