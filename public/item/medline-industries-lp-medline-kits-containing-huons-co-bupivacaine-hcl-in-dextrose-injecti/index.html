Recall

Medline Industries, LP: Medline kits containing Huons Co. Bupivacaine HCL in Dextrose Injection, USP, labeled as: 1) BLOCK TRAY, Medline Kit SKU DYNJRA1181C; 2) SPI Recall

Medline kits were distributed containing Huons Bupivacaine Hydrochloride in Dextrose Injection, USP, which was subsequently recalled due to quality issues, including microbiology testing data integrity concerns and reported efficacy complaints. Product details: Medline kits containing Huons Co. Bupivacaine HCL in Dextrose Injection, USP, labeled as: 1) BLOCK TRAY, Medline Kit SKU DYNJRA1181C; 2) SPINAL BLOCK 25G WHTCRE 5S BU, Medline Kit SKu DYNJRA1860.
